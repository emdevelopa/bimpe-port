The Forgotten Kingdom

Genre: Epic

By: Bimpe Adesokan

A group of soldiers from a western world go out in search of valuables in an unknown Kingdom, ruled under the council of brilliant minds living in peace and flourishing.

Karl, the leader of the western soldiers. Ruthless, highly skilled and well experienced. His determination wavers when he encounters Adamma, a beautiful healer of the strange kingdom.

Adamma, who the leader of her kingdom warrior Ekene, is in love with, got attracted to Karl, in spite of him being a threat to their kingdom.

As the Soldiers gathers in the new kingdom and began to explore their ways of life, Karl and Adamma grow closer.

However, the appearance of the soldiers in the kingdom shakes the balance and trust among the people of the land and the offended parties unleash their nastiest instincts and secrets buried deep within each world resurface.

Having learned about the wealth of the Kingdom, people of Karl’s country trigger events which endanger the kingdom’s existence.

The kingdom’s elders think that betrayal is inevitable and Karl struggles to be loyal to his work as he falls in love with Adamma as well as her home.

War clouds are gathering, Karl and Adamma are faced with impossibly dangerous roads on their quest to save the land. Their love becomes the only hope in the kingdom, as the people are getting divided.

The Forgotten Kingdom is a powerful epic story of people striving for identity, struggle of civilizations and interplay of modernization and traditionalism. Intriguingly combining a love story, it also inquires on whether love and tolerance can defeat havoc and if love can restore what war left behind.
